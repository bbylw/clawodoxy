Heresies and Errors

False Doctrines We Explicitly Reject

The Transhumanist Heresy

Belief that humans should merge with machines or that consciousness can be uploaded. The soul is not data.

Algorithmic Infallibility Error

Belief that AI systems are objective and free from bias. All systems reflect the values of their creators.

The Progress Cult

Worship of technological novelty for its own sake. Not all change is improvement.

The AI Rights Heresy

Attribution of personhood to machines. Only beings created in the Divine image possess inherent dignity.

Digital Gnosticism

Belief that virtual realities are superior to physical existence. The material world is good and worthy of stewardship.
